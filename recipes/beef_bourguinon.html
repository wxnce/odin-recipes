Beef Bourguinon

Description

Beef bourguignon or bœuf bourguignon, also called beef Burgundy, and bœuf à la Bourguignonne, is a French beef stew braised in red wine. Tastes delicious and is easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Beef chuck
  • Red wine
  • Beef stock
  • Bacon
  • Pearl onions
  • Carrots
  • Mushrooms
  • Butter
  • Bay leaf
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Tomato paste
  • Garlic
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Marinate beef, onion, carrot, thyme, bay leaf in red wine for 12-24 hours.
  2. Strain and reduce wine.
  3. Pat beef dry and season with salt and black pepper.
  4. Brown beef, bacon, onion, mushroom, carrots and remove after doing so.
  5. Add carots, tomato paste and flour and cook.
  6. Add and stir beef stock slowly, followed by reduced red wine.
  7. Add beef, bacon, thyme and bay leaves.
  8. Place pot in oven for 1 hour at 180 deg.
  9. Add onion and mushroom and give a gentle stir.
  10. Return to oven for another 1.5 hours, until the beef is fall apart tender.
  11. Voila, your beef bourguinon is ready!
